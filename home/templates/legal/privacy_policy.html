1. Overview

TripAI ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered travel planning service.

Our Commitment: We believe privacy is a fundamental right. We minimize data collection, use encryption, and give you control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email, name, profile picture (if provided)
  • Travel Preferences: Destinations, budget, travel style, interests
  • Trip Data: Itineraries, bookings, travel history
  • Communication: Messages with our support team and AI chatbot

Technical Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Analytics: App performance, error logs, usage patterns

Third-Party Information

When you sign in with Google or other social providers:

  • Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • OAuth tokens (encrypted and secured)

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Generate personalized travel recommendations
  • Create custom itineraries and PDF guides
  • Provide AI-powered travel assistance
  • Process and fulfill your travel requests

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns and preferences
  • Improve AI accuracy and recommendations
  • Develop new features and enhancements
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance

Communication

  • Send important service updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send promotional content (opt-in only)

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our terms of service
  • Respond to legal requests

4. Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our service (hosting, analytics, support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Safety & Security: To prevent fraud, abuse, or protect user safety
  • Business Transfers: If we're acquired or merged (with notice to you)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing

5. Data Security

Our Security Measures

Encryption

Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)

Access Control

Strict access controls and multi-factor authentication

Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring and incident response

Updates

Regular security audits and system updates

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account + 90 days
  • Trip Data: 7 years for travel records and preferences
  • Usage Analytics: 2 years in aggregated, anonymized form
  • Legal Records: As required by applicable law
Note: Some information may be retained longer if required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

TripAI operates globally and may transfer your information to countries outside your residence:

  • Legal Basis: We rely on adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or your consent
  • Safeguards: We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data during transfers
  • Your Rights: You can request information about transfers and object to them
Primary Data Centers:

United States (AWS/Google Cloud), European Union (GDPR compliant)

10. Children's Privacy

Age Restrictions

TripAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

For Parents

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@tripai.com and we will delete such information.

Teens (13-17)

Users between 13-17 should have parental permission before creating an account or providing personal information.
